## Why

The framework contributor briefing mixes durable framework orientation and
test-harness guidance with stale local paths, superseded contribution protocol,
and model-specific instructions.  Contributors need a concise, accurate
framework-owned reference while the shop remains the sole owner of agent
workflow.

## What Changes

- Rewrite `docs/contributor-briefing.md` as framework-owned orientation and
  verification guidance.
- Preserve the framework layout, meta-project harness, red-first evidence, and
  runtime prerequisites without prescribing shop lifecycle or publication
  decisions.
- Remove stale absolute paths, editable-install assumptions, example canary,
  commit/co-author directions, and obsolete contribution workflow.

## Capabilities

### New Capabilities

- `framework-contributor-guidance`: Framework-owned orientation and verification
  guidance for contributors.

### Modified Capabilities

None.

## Impact

- `docs/contributor-briefing.md` is rewritten.
- No public API, executable behavior, dependency, or baseline-spec changes.
